---
title: "Travel Planner"
---

The Travel Planner lets you search flights, compare options, and organize trip details through simple conversation with Samantha.

## **What the Travel Planner Does**

With the Travel Planner, you can:

- Search flight availability
- Compare prices and timings
- Adjust travel dates and preferences
- Store trip details in one place
- Create packing lists and reminders

All planning happens inside Mysamantha, without switching apps or tabs.

## **Search Flights**

You can search for flights using natural language.

Examples:

- “Find flights from Chennai to Delhi this weekend.”
- “Show the cheapest round trip to Dubai next month.”
- “Look for morning flights to Bangalore on Friday.”

Samantha checks current availability and pricing and returns relevant options.

## **Compare Flight Options**

Samantha helps you compare:

- Ticket prices
- Airlines
- Departure and arrival times
- Non-stop vs layover flights
- Return options (if applicable)

Results are shown clearly without ads or extra clutter.

## **Update or Refine Your Search**

You can refine results without restarting.

Examples:

- “Show evening flights instead.”
- “Try next Thursday.”
- “Only non-stop flights.”
- “Find cheaper options.”

Samantha updates the results based on your request.

## **Create a Trip Plan**

After selecting flights, you can organize the trip.

Samantha can:

- Create a **Trip Details** note
- Save flight and booking information
- Add travel dates to your calendar
- Create packing lists
- Set travel-related reminders
- Organize basic itinerary notes

All trip information stays connected in one place.

## **Store Travel Documents**

You can store and retrieve:

- Boarding passes
- Hotel confirmations
- Travel insurance
- Visa documents
- Booking details

Examples:

- “Save this under my Trip Details.”
- “Attach this boarding pass to my trip note.”

## **Packing Lists**

You can create packing lists manually or by asking Samantha.

Example:

- “Create a packing list for my trip.”

Lists can include sections such as:

- Clothes
- Toiletries
- Tech
- Travel essentials
- Medications

## **Travel Reminders**

You can set reminders without manual scheduling.

Examples:

- “Remind me to check in tomorrow at 10.”
- “Alert me two hours before airport departure.”
- “Set a hotel checkout reminder for Sunday.”

Samantha handles timing automatically.

## **Retrieve Trip Information**

You can access saved details anytime.

Examples:

- “Open my Trip Details.”
- “Show my return flight.”
- “Find my hotel confirmation.”

## **Using Maps With Travel Planning**

When location access is enabled, Samantha can:

- Estimate travel time
- Find nearby places
- Add locations to travel plans
- Reference places mentioned in notes or messages

## **Offline Behavior**

- Saved notes, to-dos, and trip details are available offline.
- Flight search, pricing, and maps require internet access.
- Data syncs automatically when you reconnect.

## **Privacy & Control**

- Samantha only performs actions you request.
- Travel data is encrypted.
- Your data is never sold or used to train AI models.
- You control what is saved and organized.
