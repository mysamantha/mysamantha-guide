title: "Web Clipper"
---

The Mysamantha browser extension lets you record meetings, clip webpages, take notes, and add screenshots without leaving your current tab.

Everything you capture is saved directly into your **Mysamantha Notes**, so your ideas, research, and recordings stay organized in one place.

## What the Extension Can Do

With the Mysamantha extension, you can:

- Record meetings with a microphone and screen audio
- Capture full webpages or selected elements
- Clip content from multiple tabs into a single note
- Create notes directly from the extension
- Take and attach screenshots instantly
- Save everything directly into your Mysamantha workspace

## Meeting Capture

### What it does

Meeting Capture records **microphone and screen audio** directly from your browser.\
You can also take **screenshots during the session** and attach them to the same note.

After recording, you can upload the capture for transcription or download the file.

### How to use

1. Open the Mysamantha extension
2. Go to **Meeting Capture**
3. Start recording
4. Take screenshots anytime during the session
5. Stop recording
6. Review the duration and file size
7. Upload for transcription or download the recording

### Use cases

- Online meetings
- Lectures
- Interviews
- Product demos
- Discussions you want to revisit later

### Why it matters

Your recordings, transcriptions, and screenshots stay together in a single note, so nothing gets lost or scattered.

## Web Clipper (Full Page + Selected Elements)

### What it does

The Web Clipper lets you save **entire webpages** or capture **specific elements** from a page as notes.

### How to use

**Save a full page**

1. Open the extension
2. Click **Save Full Page**
3. Preview the content
4. Save as a new note
5. Edit the note title if needed

**Clip selected elements**

1. Click **Start Clipping**
2. Select elements on the page
3. Save to a new note or an existing one
4. Edit the note title before saving

### Use cases

- Saving articles and documentation
- Collecting highlights and references
- Storing long-form research material

### Why it matters

You can capture both full pages and specific content using one tool, without copying, pasting, or bookmarking manually.

## Multi-Tab Clipping

### What it does

Multi-Tab Clipping lets you collect content from **multiple browser tabs** and save everything into **one single note**.

### How to enable

1. Go to **Profile → Settings → Web Clipper**
2. Enable **Multi-Tab Clipping**

### How it works

1. Start clipping on one webpage
2. Switch to another browser tab
3. Continue selecting elements
4. Save all selected content together into one note

### Use cases

- Research across multiple websites
- Comparisons
- Grouped references and resources

### Why it matters

Related content from different sources stays organized in one place instead of being split across multiple notes.

## Notes and Screenshots

### What it does

You can create notes directly from the extension and attach screenshots instantly.

### How to use

1. Open the **Notes** tab in the extension
2. Write your note
3. Add screenshots
4. Save

### Use cases

- Quick ideas while browsing
- Visual references
- Contextual notes tied to webpages

### Why it matters

You capture thoughts and visuals without switching apps or breaking your flow.

## Where Everything Is Saved

- All recordings, clips, screenshots, and notes are saved as **Notes**
- Notes created from the extension behave exactly like notes created in the app
- You can edit, label, pin, move, or delete them anytime
- Everything syncs automatically across your Mysamantha workspace
