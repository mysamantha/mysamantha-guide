---
title: "Web Clipper"
---

The Mysamantha browser extension lets you save content from the web directly into your notes.\
 You can capture full pages, selected sections, or meeting transcripts without breaking your flow.

Everything you save becomes a note you can organize or act on later.

## **What the Web Clipper Does**

The Web Clipper allows you to:

- Save entire web pages
- Capture selected text or highlights
- Add content to new or existing notes
- Record meetings and generate transcripts
- Keep research, ideas, and references in one place

All clipped or recorded content is stored inside **Notes**.

## **One-Click Full Page Capture**

### **What it does**

Saves the entire content of a web page as a note.

### **How to use**

1. Open any web page
2. Click the Mysamantha extension
3. Select **Full Page**

The page is saved automatically with a generated title and category.

### **Use case**

Useful for saving articles, documentation, research papers, or long reads.

### **Why it matters**

You don’t lose important content and don’t need to copy or bookmark pages manually.

## **Selective Capture (Snippets & Highlights)**

### **What it does**

Lets you save specific sections or highlights from a page.

### **How to use**

1. Highlight text on a web page
2. Click the Mysamantha extension
3. Choose to save as a new note or add to an existing note

Titles and categories are applied automatically.

### **Use case**

Helpful when skimming long articles or collecting key points.

### **Why it matters**

You save only what’s relevant and keep notes focused and actionable.

## **Adding to Existing Notes**

### **What it does**

Allows you to append web content to notes you already have.

### **How it works**

- Select text on a page
- Choose an existing note when clipping
- The content is added directly

This helps build research or idea notes over time.

## **Meeting Capture**

### **What it does**

Meeting Capture lets you record meetings and generate transcripts directly from the extension.

### **How to use**

1. Open the Mysamantha extension
2. Select **Meeting Capture**
3. Start recording the meeting
4. Stop recording when finished

A transcript is generated and saved automatically as a note.

### **Use case**

Useful for:

- Team meetings
- Interviews
- Online calls
- Reviews or discussions you want to revisit later

### **Why it matters**

You don’t have to take notes while listening.\
 Meetings are captured accurately and stored for later reference.

## **Where Clipped and Recorded Content Goes**

- All clipped pages, snippets, and transcripts are saved as **Notes**
- Auto titles are generated for full-page captures and transcripts
- You can edit, label, pin, or move these notes like any other note
- There’s no difference between clipped notes and manually created notes

## **Offline Behavior**

- The Web Clipper and Meeting Capture require an internet connection to save content
- Saved notes and transcripts can be viewed and edited offline
- Changes sync automatically when you reconnect

## **Privacy & Control**

- Only the content you choose is saved
- Data is encrypted
- Clipped pages and transcripts are never sold or used to train AI models
- You can delete clipped notes or transcripts anytime
