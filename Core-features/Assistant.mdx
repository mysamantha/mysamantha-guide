title: "Assistant"
---

Samantha is your in-app assistant in [mysamantha.ai](http://mysamantha.ai).\
She helps you create, find, and act on information across notes, to dos, journals, email and calendar.

Samantha acts only when you ask.

## **Talking to Samantha**

You can interact with Samantha in natural language.

You can:

- Type messages
- Use voice input
- Continue conversations over time

You don’t need special commands or formats.

## **What Samantha Can Do**

Samantha helps you manage work and life from one place.

**She can:**

- Create notes, to dos, and journal entries
- Edit or summarize existing content
- Turn conversations into actions
- Retrieve past notes, tasks, or entries
- Coordinate actions across email, calendar, and maps (when connected)

## **Conversations to Actions**

**What it does**\
Turns chat messages into structured actions.

**How it works**

- You describe what you want
- Samantha identifies intent
- The right action is created automatically

**Examples:**

- A message becomes a note
- A plan becomes a to dos
- A conversation becomes a reminder or an event

## **Memory & Context**

**What it does**\
Maintains context across conversations.

**Samantha can remember:**

- Previous chats
- Related notes and tasks
- Past decisions and references

This allows follow-ups without repeating yourself.

## **Smart Recall**

**What it does**\
Finds relevant past information when needed.

**How it works**

- Samantha searches your notes, journals, tasks, and chats
- Relevant context is surfaced automatically

This helps maintain continuity over time.

## **Email, Calendar, and Maps Support**

When integrations are connected, Samantha can:

- Summarize and act on emails
- Schedule, move, or review calendar events
- Use location context for planning

All integrations are optional and permission-based.

## **Writing and Drafting**

**What it does**\
Helps write content with review control.

**Samantha can:**

- Draft emails
- Rewrite or refine text
- Generate summaries or explanations

Nothing is sent or saved without your approval.

## **Offline Behavior**

When offline:

- Notes, to do, and journals still work
- Samantha chat and AI actions are unavailable

Everything syncs once you reconnect.

## **Privacy & Control**

- Samantha only acts on your request
- Data is encrypted
- Content is not sold or used to train AI models
- Integrations can be disconnected at any time

You remain in control of every action. [more](https://mysamantha.ai/privacy)

<Note>
  Samantha is not a chatbot. She’s a personal assistant who helps you with notes, tasks, reflections, and plans - all in one place.

  Samantha helps organize, remember, and act when you ask.
</Note>
