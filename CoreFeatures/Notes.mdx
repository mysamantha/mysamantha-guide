title: "Notes"
---

Notes in [mysamantha.ai](http://mysamantha.ai) help you capture, organize, and act on information without switching tools.\
You can write, speak, attach files, and turn notes into actions when needed.

Notes work offline.\
AI-powered actions require an internet connection.

## **Creating Notes**

You can create notes in multiple ways:

- Type directly in the Notes section
- Use voice to record and transcribe thoughts
- Ask Samantha to create a note from chat

## **Voice to Note**

**What it does**\
Converts spoken input into written notes.

**How to use**

- Tap the microphone
- Speak naturally
- Your note is transcribed and saved automatically

Free users can record up to **60 seconds per note**.\
Paid users can record up to **60 minutes per note**.

## **Auto Titles**

**What it does**\
Automatically generates a clear title based on the note’s content.

**How it works**

- When you save a note, a title is created for you
- You can edit the title anytime

This keeps notes easy to find without manual naming.

## **Auto Categorization**

**What it does**\
Assigns notes to smart categories based on content.

**Categories include**

- Work
- Personal
- Academics
- Health & Fitness
- Finance
- Social & Events
- Ideas & Creativity
- Home & Lifestyle

You can move notes between categories anytime.

## **Labels**

**What it does**\
Lets you organize notes using custom labels.

**How to use**

- Create your own labels
- Apply one or more labels to a note
- Search or filter notes using labels

Labels help when you manage multiple projects or topics.

## **Dynamic AI Insights**

### **What it does**

Transforms your notes into intelligent outcomes instantly. Just highlight any text and ask Samantha to refine, rewrite, or generate something new. There are no fixed actions. You can request anything, from polishing ideas to creating entirely new formats.

### **What you can do**

- Summarize key points
- Turn content into To-Dos
- Draft emails directly from notes
- Explain or simplify complex text
- Create meeting recaps
- Enhance or polish writing
- Rewrite in different tones or styles
- Generate creative outputs like poems or alternate versions
- Ask anything related to your highlighted content

### **How to use**

1. Highlight text inside a note
2. Type what you want Samantha to do
3. The content transforms instantly

## Chat with Notes

**What it is**\
Chat with Notes lets you talk directly with your notes using Samantha. Instead of rereading everything, you can ask questions, get insights, and turn ideas into actions instantly.

### What you can do

- Turn meeting notes into follow-up messages
- Identify decisions, action points, or missing details
- Rewrite content in a clearer or more professional tone
- Break plans into step-by-step actions
- Extract deadlines, tasks, or key information
- Simplify complex ideas
- Brainstorm or expand on your existing notes

### How to use

1. Open any note.
2. Tap the **Notes icon** in the top-right corner.
3. Start chatting with Samantha.
4. Ask anything -  Samantha understands your note’s context automatically.

## **Pin Notes**

**What it does**\
Keeps important notes at the top.

**How to use**

- Pin a note
- It stays visible above others in its category

Pinned notes are useful for ongoing or critical information.

## **Attachments**

**What it does**\
Allows adding files directly inside notes.

**Supported attachments**

- Images
- Audio recordings
- Documents

Attachments stay linked to the note so everything related stays together.

## **Export & Share**

**What it does**\
Converts notes into shareable formats.

**Available formats**

- PDF
- HTML

**How to use**

- Select a note
- Choose Export
- Download as PDF or HTML.

## **Import from Notion**

**What it does**\
Brings existing Notion notes into [mysamantha.ai](http://mysamantha.ai).

**How to use**

- Open Notes
- Click the kebab menu
- Select **Import from Notion**

Your content is imported without manual copying.

## **Using Notes with Samantha**

You can manage notes directly from chat.

**Examples**

- “Create a note from this.”
- “Summarize this note.”
- “Turn this into a to do.”
- “Open my meeting notes.”

Samantha only acts when you ask.

## **Offline Behavior**

When offline, you can:

- Create new notes
- Edit existing notes
- Organize, pin, and label notes

Smart Actions and assistant features resume once you’re back online.

## **Privacy & Control**

- Notes are encrypted
- Your data is never sold
- Notes are not used to train AI models
- You can export or delete notes anytime. [more](https://mysamantha.ai/privacy)
