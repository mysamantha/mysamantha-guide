title: "AI Credits"
---

AI Credits power Samantha’s AI features while keeping [mysamantha.ai](http://mysamantha.ai) free and accessible.\
You only spend credits when you actively use AI.

## What Are AI Credits?

AI Credits are tokens used when you interact with **Samantha**, the AI assistant, or trigger AI-powered actions.

Core features like **Notes, To-Do, and Journals** are free unless an AI action is used.

## How AI Credits Are Used

AI Credits are consumed based on the type of AI interaction:

- **Text chat with Samantha** → **1 AI Credit**
- **Voice chat with Samantha** → **2 AI Credits**
- **Smart Actions in Notes** (summarize, todo, explain, email, etc.) → **1 AI Credit per action**
- **Voice transcription** → **2 AI Credits per transcription**

## AI Credits for Free Users

Free users get a daily allowance to explore AI features:

- **20 AI Credits per day**
- Available for the **first 5 days**

## AI Credits for Pro and Lifetime Users

Pro and Lifetime plans include:

- **\*Unlimited AI Credits**

<Note>
  ( \*  )  -  Unlimited usage is subject to a [fair usage policy](https://mysamantha.ai/terms#fair-usage-policy) to prevent abuse.
</Note>
