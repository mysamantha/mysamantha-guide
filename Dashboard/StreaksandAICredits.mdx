---
title: "Streaks And AI Credits"
---

Streaks and AI Credits are designed to reward consistent use of [mysamantha.ai](http://mysamantha.ai) while keeping the core app free and accessible.

## **AI Credits**

**What they are**\
 AI Credits are tokens used when you interact with Samantha.

They apply only to AI-powered features.

### **How AI Credits Are Used**

- Each chat with Samantha uses **1 AI Credit**
- Voice transcription uses **2 AI Credits**
- Notes, To Do, and Journals are free to use

You only spend credits when using AI features.

## **Earning AI Credits with Streaks**

You can earn free AI Credits by maintaining a daily usage streak.

### **Daily Streak Rewards**

- Earn **5 AI Credits** for each consecutive day of use
- Receive a **50 AI Credit bonus on Day 30**

A full 30-day streak gives you:

- **150 AI Credits** from daily rewards
- **50 bonus AI Credits**
- **200 AI Credits total**

## **Breaking and Restarting a Streak**

- If you miss a day, the streak resets
- You can start a new streak anytime
- There is no penalty for restarting

Streaks are meant to encourage consistency, not pressure.

## **Long-Term Streaks**

There is no upper limit to streaks.

Some users maintain streaks for hundreds of days by using the app regularly.

Rewards continue as long as you stay consistent.
