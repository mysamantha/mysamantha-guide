To Do in Mysamantha is your daily command center. It's not just a task list. Samantha helps you create tasks from conversation, set up recurring routines, and stay on top of what actually matters each day.

## What you can do with To Do

- **Create tasks instantly.** Type them in, or just tell Samantha what needs doing and she'll add them for you.
- **Set due dates and reminders** so nothing slips through.
- **Build recurring tasks** for habits and routines you repeat daily, weekly, or monthly.
- **Prioritize** with urgency levels so you always know what to tackle first.
- **Break down big tasks** into subtasks to keep complex work manageable.
- **Link tasks to notes** so context and action live in the same place.
- **Ask Samantha** to plan your day, surface what's overdue, or draft a schedule from your task list.

## Who it's for

To Do is for people who want more than a checklist. If you need your task manager to understand context, help you plan, and handle your routines automatically, this is it.
