## **MySamantha and Samantha**

MySamantha is your digital workspace where all your thoughts, ideas, and plans stay connected. Samantha is the intelligent assistant inside the app who helps you understand what you think, stay organized, and bring clarity into your day. MySamantha gives you the space, and Samantha gives you the support.
